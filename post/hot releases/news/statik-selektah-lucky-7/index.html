Statik Selektah has dropped his seventh album, Lucky 7!

Joined by a mix of veterans and up and coming MCs, Lucky 7 _is a star-studded album that showcases Statik’s hustle. The album features classic East Coast production that has brought the respected producer success. Cop _Lucky 7_ today and hear collabs with the likes of _Joey Bada$$, Action Bronson, Royce Da 5’9″, Big K.R.I.T., Mick Jenkins, Ab-Soul, Sean Price and so many others.

Buy Lucky 7 now!
