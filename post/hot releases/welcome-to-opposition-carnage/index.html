Carnage x G-Eazy Release Step Brothers Mixtape

Hot Releases

May 10, 2017

**2016 proved to be a game-changing year for Carnage, pumping out collabs with Lil Yachty, Young Thug, and the release of What Do You Want with Lil Uzi, ASAP Ferg, and Rich The Kid. Keeping the hype alive, Carnage recently dropped Step Brothers, a fire EP with G-Eazy.  Check out the newly released music video for their song Down For Me featuring 24hrs above. Their video for Guala definitely got our attention but this time they’re showing us the darker side of their rising fame with the gritty video directed by Edgar Esteves. **
